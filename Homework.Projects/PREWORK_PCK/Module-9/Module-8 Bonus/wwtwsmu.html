Bio

Once Described by Darryl Smyers of the Dallas Observer as, “Loud and relatively complex, the songs feature a nice, tongue-in-cheek dose of humor and humility with some Emo-tinged vocals that are pretty impressive.” Werewolf Therewolf are ready to take those accolades once step further with the Formations EP.

The refined groove of the new EP’s opening cut, “Praise the Sun” highlights the new focus the band has honed these past 7 years. Tuneful, catchy, but still retaining that necessary edge, “Praise the Sun” is The Walkmen filtered through mid-period Metallica. Anthemic without being pretentious, Formations is the sound of a band reaching its peak, priming for a larger audience.
